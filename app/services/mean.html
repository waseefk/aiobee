MEAN STACK Application Development

What is MEAN?

  • MEAN is an Acronym of Mongo DB, Express JS, Angular and Node JS.
  • All composite of Javascript which makes them to stand apart in other frameworks.
  • One Language for all Server, Client and Databse.
  • Your application database is stored in Mongo DB which is NoSQL Database.
  • Your application APIs are created in Express JS.
  • Your application server configuration and networks are configured in Node JS
  • Your application front end framework would be blazing fast Angular.

Why MEAN?

  • MEAN is based on One Language - One Complate Package.
  • Its an entire package for building great website and web applications.
  • Its very easy to maintain, collaborate with your team.
  • You can deploy in any Operating System like Unix, Linux, Windows or even in Mac Os X.

How MEAN Helps?

  • MEAN application helps your customers feel more happy on the speed it loads as they search your application and load it in the browser.
  • The faster it loads the most happy is the customer.
  • It not only loads faster, once it is loaded after that there would be no reload of the page.
